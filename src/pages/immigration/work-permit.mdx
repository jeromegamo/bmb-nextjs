# Work Permit

If you already have a job offer or LMIA, BMB Immigration Services can assist you to complete your documentation with speed and reliability.

For a quick guide to what you will need please see below:

-  Application form for Work Permit Outside of Canada
-  Two (2) Passport Photographs
-  Family Information Form (IMM 5645)
-  Processing Fee
-  Your Job Offer or contract from the prospective employer
-  A copy of the Labour Market Impact Assessment (LMIA) provided by ESDC
-  A Valid Passport
-  Evidence that you meet the requirements of the job being offered such as employment references and professional qualifications
-  Copies of Educational Certificates
-  Proof of financial support to cover the expenses for the duration of your visit
